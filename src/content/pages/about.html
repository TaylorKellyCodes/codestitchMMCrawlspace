About MM Crawlspace Renovations

Protecting Triangle Homes From the Ground Up

or homeowners in Raleigh, Cary, Apex, and Durham, the crawl space is often out of sight and out of mind—until it isn’t. Between North Carolina’s relentless humidity and the unique drainage challenges of our local clay soil, your foundation is under constant attack from moisture and mold.

MM Crawlspace Renovations was founded to provide a higher standard of service. We don't believe in "one-size-fits-all" liners or aggressive sales tactics. We believe in Trustworthy Work. Our team specializes in high-performance encapsulation, precision mold remediation, and code-compliant prebatt and fire-stop installations. We treat every home as if it were our own, ensuring your air is clean, your floors are warm, and your foundation is bone-dry.

Trustworthy work isn't just a slogan for us; it’s the standard. We show up on time, we do what we say we’re going to do, and we make sure your home is safer than when we arrived. Miguel Martinez Founder
