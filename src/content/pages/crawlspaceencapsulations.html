Foundation Protection

Stop Raleigh’s Humidity From Destroying Your Home

Did you know that up to 50% of the air you breathe on the first floor of your home comes directly from your crawl space? In the Triangle, our high humidity and red clay soil create a "perfect storm" for moisture buildup, wood rot, and unhealthy air.

Our encapsulation process transforms your damp, dark crawl space into a clean, dry, and conditioned environment. By sealing the floor, walls, and vents, we stop the "Stack Effect," preventing humid air and soil gases from rising into your living space. This doesn't just protect your foundation; it lowers your energy bills and improves your family’s respiratory health.

Our Trustworthy Process

  • Initial Inspection & Assessment: We analyze moisture levels, check for structural rot, and identify any active mold colonies in your Raleigh or Durham home.
  • Debris Removal & Leveling: We clear out old insulation and trash, then level the soil to ensure a smooth, professional vapor barrier fit.
  • Premium Sealing: We install a heavy-duty, 20-mil antimicrobial vapor barrier, meticulously sealing all seams and wrapping every pier.
  • Environmental Control: We install a high-efficiency crawl space dehumidifier and a dedicated monitoring system so you can track your humidity levels from your phone.
