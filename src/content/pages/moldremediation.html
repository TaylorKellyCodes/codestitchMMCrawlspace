Expert Mold Removal for a Healthier Triangle Home

Discovering mold in your crawl space is more than just a maintenance issue—it’s a health risk. In the humid North Carolina climate, mold spores can thrive on damp floor joists and insulation. Because of the "Stack Effect," those spores are pulled upward into your living areas, potentially causing respiratory issues and persistent musty odors.

At MM Crawlspace Renovations, we don't just "spray and walk away." We provide comprehensive mold remediation that follows strict safety protocols to ensure your home is clean and your air is breathable. Our team identifies the specific type of growth and, more importantly, stops the moisture source to ensure the mold never returns.

Our Remediation Process

  • Detailed Assessment: We inspect the extent of the growth and use moisture meters to find the "hot spots" where fungi are thriving.
  • HEPA Containment & Removal: Using advanced HEPA filtration and vacuuming, we safely remove active mold colonies without cross-contaminating the rest of your home.
  • Antimicrobial Treatment: We apply professional-grade, eco-friendly antimicrobial solutions to kill remaining spores and prevent future regrowth on wood surfaces.
  • Root Cause Correction: We address the "Why." Whether you need a Sump Pump or a full Encapsulation, we ensure the environment is no longer hospitable to mold.
